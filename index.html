Featured work

Building an invoice-tracking workflow for a fuel distribution network.

Juliet Petroleum

Fuel distribution · B2B invoicing

The problem

Invoices between individual filling stations and corporate customers were regularly misplaced or simply never submitted to the customer's accounts team. That created payment delays and gaps in reporting between the stations and corporate accounts.

The solution

I built an automated workflow that logs every station sale as an invoice, automatically notifies the corporate customer's accountant when a new invoice is issued, and sends a confirmation update once payment is received — so no invoice depends on someone remembering to send it.
